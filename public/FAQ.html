Frequently Asked Questions

What are your restaurant's hours of operation?

We are open every day from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Join us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! We have a variety of vegan and vegetarian options on our menu, including salads, wraps, and plant-based dishes.

Can I make a reservation in advance?

Yes, you can make a reservation by calling us or through our website. We recommend reserving in advance for weekends and holidays.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we provide delivery services through our partner delivery apps. You can place an order online for home delivery.

Is your restaurant wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible. We also have designated spaces for wheelchair users.

Do you cater for special dietary requirements?

Absolutely! We cater to a range of dietary requirements including gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free options. Just inform us when placing your order.

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space available for our customers. You can park in our dedicated parking lot next to the restaurant.
